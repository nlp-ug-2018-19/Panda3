This Chatbot was created as a part of a final project of the Web Development course at the University of Gdańsk.

The task of our program is to inform potential users about the current weather in selected places in the world.
Our Panda will provide information on:

Potential overcast

Current temperature

Possible rainfall

Strong gusts of wind

Armospheric pressure

Possible fog

Possible snowfall
