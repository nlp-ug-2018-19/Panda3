How to use our Chatbot.

1. Make sure you are currently using the Google Chrome browser.

Image credits: iscreamsundae.com

2. Press the "Ask Weatherbot" button and ask abut the weather in a city in which you want to check it. Provided below are some exemplatory quenstions you may ask our Weatherbot.

Examplatory questions:

What's the temperature in London today?
Is humidity in Paris high today?
How strong is the wind in Vienna?
Is there fog in Berlin?
What is the pressure in Warsaw?
What's the weather in Kyoto?

Make sure you do not forget the name of a city in which weather you are interested in.

3. You will hear and see the data about the weather in that city on that day.

Most frequently asked questions:

1. What to do when you don't get the anticipated answer:

Please try rephrasing your question, e.g. instead of "Is it foggy in Amsterdam today?" please try "Is there fog in Amsterdam today?"
