How to use our Chatbot.

1. Make sure you are currently using the Google Chrome browser.

Image credits: iscreamsundae.com

2. Press the "Ask Weatherbot" button and say one of the listed below key words and the name of a city in which you want to check the weather.
Key words:

temperature
humidity
wind
fog
pressure
weather

Make sure that you provide the city name along with one of these keywords.

3. You will hear and see the data about the weather in that city on that day.
